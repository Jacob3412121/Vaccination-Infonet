Are Vaccines Safe?

Vaccines are extensively tested and monitored for safety. Most side effects are minor, such as soreness or low-grade fever, and serious reactions are rare.

Do Vaccines Weaken the Immune System?

No, vaccines do not weaken the immune system. They help it learn to fight specific infections.

Can Multiple Vaccines Be Given at Once?

Yes, multiple vaccines can be given simultaneously without negative effects on the immune system.
 

Why Vaccines Against Diseases Not Common in My Area?

Vaccines protect against diseases that can spread globally. Even if a disease is uncommon locally, it can still be introduced through travel.

Is Natural Immunity Better Than Vaccine-Acquired Immunity?

Vaccine-acquired immunity is often safer and more reliable than natural immunity, which can come with serious health risks.
